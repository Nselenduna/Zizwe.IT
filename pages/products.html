Ezethu izitori

Social Connection in Your Mother Tongue
Building digital spaces for Nguni language speakers. Community matters, and so does being able to connect in the language of your heart.

The Vision

Social media platforms are designed for English and other major languages. For Ndebele, Zulu, Xhosa, and Swati speakers, expressing yourself online often means switching languages or losing nuance.

Ezethu izitori is a social platform written entirely in Nguni languages, designed for the way we actually communicate.

Who It's For

  • Ndebele speakers
  • Zulu speakers
  • Xhosa speakers
  • Swati speakers
  • Anyone who wants to connect in their mother tongue
